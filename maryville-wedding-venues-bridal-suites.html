Wedding stay FAQ

Questions couples and families ask before booking

Can the Broadway lofts work as bridal suites?

Yes. They are best for getting ready, detail photos, hair and makeup, and having a calm downtown place before the ceremony or reception.

Which units are best for wedding guests?

Greenway Village is usually strongest for families or groups because the units have two bedrooms, two bathrooms, and more space to spread out.

Yes. The Broadway lofts are directly downtown above Bella Restaurant, close to Capitol Theatre, restaurants, shops, coffee, and the Broadway corridor.
