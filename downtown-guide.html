New downtown Maryville entertainment venue

The Armory Maryville is one of the newest reasons to plan a downtown night

If you are Googling Maryville Armory, Downtown Maryville Armory, or things to do in downtown Maryville, this is the new venue to have on your radar. The Armory at 496 Ellis Avenue is a restaurant, comfortable bar, and entertainment destination with duckpin bowling, digital darts, immersive games, mini golf, yard games, a 4,500-square-foot rooftop deck, and a 1,000-square-foot patio.

It fits date nights, family plans, birthday parties, rehearsal dinners, corporate gatherings, and bigger friend-group weekends. From a Bricks Over Broadway stay, it is an easy add-on to a downtown Maryville itinerary: eat on Broadway, play at The Armory, then come back to a walkable local base.

Family fun

Greenbelt, picnic lunch, library, ice cream, comics

  1. Walk or bike the Greenbelt with Maryville Bike & Trail nearby.
  2. Pick up lunch from Foothills Milling Cafe or Los Amigos for an easy picnic.
  3. Use the Blount County Public Library, Capitol Theatre ice cream, and The Golden Age 1942 as low-stress stops.

Greenway Village

Cookies, ice cream, coffee, dinner, indoor golf, and Greenbelt access

  1. Start with sweets downstairs at The Great American Cookie Company and Marble Slab Creamery, owned locally by one of the Greenway Village stay owners.
  2. Use Southern Grace Coffee Co. for coffee, breakfast, and an easy morning meet-up close to the suites.
  3. Plan dinner at DSB Provisions, then keep the night fun with Trackman golf and lounge time at The FoxHole.
  4. Walk straight onto the Greenway for a low-stress route through downtown Maryville.

Rainy day

Dandy Lions, coffee, galleries, and a show

  1. Start with coffee and a snack at Southern Grace Coffee Co.
  2. Make Dandy Lions the featured shopping stop for gifts, accessories, and locally owned downtown browsing.
  3. Add Village Tinker, The Boutique on Broadway, or Pokeys if you want a longer shop loop.
  4. Keep the afternoon flexible with galleries or a performance at the Clayton Center.

Quick answers

What guests usually need to know

What is The Armory Maryville?

The Armory Maryville is a restaurant, bar, and entertainment venue at 496 Ellis Avenue with duckpin bowling, digital darts, immersive games, mini golf, yard games, a rooftop deck, patio space, and private-event options.

Is The Armory close to downtown Maryville lodging?

Yes. The Armory is a useful addition to a downtown Maryville stay, especially for guests planning dinner, games, rooftop drinks, birthdays, rehearsal dinners, corporate gatherings, or group weekends.

Is downtown Maryville walkable

Yes. Broadway restaurants, shops, coffee, the theatre district, Greenbelt access, and Pistol Creek are all useful on foot.

Where should I check current hours

Use Downtown Maryville's official business directory and event calendar. Restaurants, shops, music, and seasonal events change.

Can Maryville work as a regional base

Yes. Guests use downtown Maryville for Alcoa, McGhee Tyson Airport, Maryville College, Knoxville, Townsend, and Smoky Mountain day trips.
